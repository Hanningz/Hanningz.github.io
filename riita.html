Riita

Role: UX researcher, UX Designer
When: 11.2016 to 03.2017

During my course “Human-Centered Design Project” at the Tampere University of Technology, my team worked on a topic about Recycling, and we came up with an interactive prototype solution of recycling network system named Riita, a platform to encourage local citizens (Tampere) to recycle and provide more helpful information. Our work included gaining user insights mainly by Contextual Inquiry, after analyzing data, we are working on a solution in an iterate design process. Since this project is still going on, I will update more information when finished.















Talk to people

We divided users into three categories, family, foreigner and pet owner, then 3 contextual inquiries were conducted for groups (one for each). Meanwhile, we had interviews for three groups, especially we interviewed three more foreigner students in university early. In addition, one group member visited the local recycling center in Tampere.








Models & Affinity notes

Based on the contextual inquiries and interview, we created consolidated models and affinity notes to dig the insights and pain points from recyclers.
