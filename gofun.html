The project is for course “User Experience: Design and Evaluation” at the Tampere University of Technology, our group chose the topic “Gamify team coding”, and we created a solution which is an application to help college students perform coding work in team with fun and higher motivation. By adding game element in application, students can find work became funnier and easier to make it under control, moreover, the application can prompt user engagement while make coding less frustrating and ineffective.















How gamification can help in team coding assignment ?

After we defined the problem, we prepared for user research, at beginning several interviews were conducted in university, soon we realized that not all students we interviewed both have coding experience and like playing game, then we just focused on interview to get a basic perspective of team coding and figure out problems, meanwhile, an online survey was created to gain more views on game side from students, and we tried to invite students from IT filed to fill it. Both were implemented by Google form.








Solving the problem

According to user interview and online survey, we extracted most popular game elements and applied them into group work, accordingly, we set the rules for players in system as follow.
