Design Thinking Workshop

When: 10.2016

The workshop was organized by Demola New Factory, to help students learn more about Design Thinking, and gain hands-on experience.











Design Process

During workshop we worked in pair to help to solve other’s problem in Design Thinking process.








Prototypes

At the end of workshop, we used physical stuffs to make prototypes based on the problem we defined before. For example, my pair was working on a project to engage young people to learn video about cancer, then I suggested to use Gamification and made prototypes (left one). Next we tested porotypes with pairs and shard them with other groups, we learned a lot from the workshop and had fun.
